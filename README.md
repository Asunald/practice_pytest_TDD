##  Evaluation

This course is **100% project-based**.

All projects are **open-ended AI projects**. The goal is not to follow rigid instructions, but to explore, design, experiment, and deliver working AI-driven solutions.

You will work **in teams of two** (fixed teams throughout the semester).

### Grading Breakdown

#### Part I — 5 AI Tool-Based Open Projects (75%)

* There will be **5 projects**
* Each project is worth **15 points**
* Total: **15 × 5 = 75 points**

Most projects require you to use **existing AI tools**.

You are generally **free to choose any AI tools**, including:

* Commercial tools
* Free tools
* Open-source tools
* Cloud APIs
* Local models
* Paid services (if you choose)

Some projects may impose limited constraints, but in most cases, tool choice is open.

The emphasis is:

> Learn to leverage AI tools effectively in real-world scenarios.
